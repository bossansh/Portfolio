The Three Little Pigs

One day, 3 little pigs decided to build their houses. The first pig made his house out of straw: The second pig made his house out of sticks: The last big made his house with bricks:

Suddenly, a big bad wolf came and decided to blow the pigs' houses. The wolf first came to the straw house. "I'm going to huff and puff and blow your house down!" the wolf screeched. "But why?" asked the pig. "Because I am big and bad!" the wolf yelled. Then, the big bad wolf huffed and puffed, and then blew the straw house down. The pig hurriedly went to the stick house.

The big bad wolf went over to the stick house. "I am once again going to huff and puff to blow this house down!" it yelled. The pig who made the stick house yelled back, "I'd like to see you try!" The wolf got really mad. It huffed and puffed so hard that the entire stick house was blown in a second. The two pigs in the stick house ran into the brick house.

The big bad wolf went over to the brick house. "This is my grand finale. I am going to blow this solid brick house down," growled the wolf. However, when the wolf huffed and puffed, it couldn't blow the house down. The pigs all laughed at the wolf. The pigs lived happily ever after.
